List of recommendations and notes about Seattle
Place Description Link
Le Caviste A French wine bar in South Lake Union, great prices and great staff. LINK →
Cafe Suliman Small Middle Eastern cafe in the Melrose Market. It's wonderful. LINK →
L'Oursin French café attitude and fare. It's a great meal, and the bar program is top notch too with one of the best collections of Calvados in the city. LINK →
Local Tide Fremont seafood spot. It's casual. It's delicious. It's consistently perfect. The shrimp toast is a must. LINK →
Espresso Vivace Espresso Vivace is a coffee roaster and cafe in Capitol Hill, with another location in South Lake Union. My favorite coffee in the city. LINK →
Temple Pastries Insane pastries. The chocolate buckwheat shortbread is my favorite. (and the cardamom almond brioche wow) LINK →
Market House Meats The best reuben sandwich in Seattle. You'll need a nap afterwards, but it is very much worth it. LINK →
